# 📜 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community for everyone.

As contributors, maintainers, and community members of **VerityAgent**, we pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

Our goal is to build a friendly, professional, and collaborative open-source AI community.

---

# 🤝 Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- ✅ Being respectful and professional
- ✅ Welcoming newcomers
- ✅ Accepting constructive feedback
- ✅ Helping others learn
- ✅ Sharing knowledge openly
- ✅ Focusing on solving problems
- ✅ Respecting different viewpoints
- ✅ Supporting collaboration
- ✅ Using inclusive language
- ✅ Giving credit to others' work

---

# 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Threats or intimidation
- Trolling or insulting others
- Spam or promotional abuse
- Sharing malicious software
- Phishing attempts
- Publishing private information without permission
- Illegal activities
- Offensive or inappropriate content
- Any behavior that creates an unsafe environment

---

# 🛡️ AI Ethics

As an AI-focused project, contributors are expected to:

- Build AI responsibly.
- Respect user privacy.
- Avoid creating harmful or malicious AI systems.
- Never intentionally develop software that promotes fraud, abuse, or illegal activities.
- Follow applicable laws and ethical AI practices.

---

# 💻 Development Guidelines

Please:

- Write clean and maintainable code.
- Follow project conventions.
- Document new features.
- Test your changes before submitting.
- Keep pull requests focused on a single topic.
- Be open to review and feedback.

---

# 📝 Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Open a private GitHub discussion or issue if appropriate.
2. Contact the project maintainer directly.
3. Include relevant details and evidence if possible.

All reports will be handled respectfully and, where appropriate, confidentially.

---

# ⚖️ Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Possible actions include:

- Friendly reminder
- Warning
- Removal of inappropriate content
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban from the project

The response will depend on the severity and frequency of the behavior.
